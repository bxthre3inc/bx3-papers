### AI Regulation Update

**Colorado AI Law (HB 23-1057):**
- Under active rewrite — Gov. Polis endorsed framework removing audit requirements, focusing on **transparency notices**
- Original law faced tech/VC opposition; xAI filed lawsuit (April 2026) challenging constitutionality
- Effective date: Original June 2026 target may shift with rewrite
- **Impact on Starting 5:** Rewrite softens disclosure burden — transparency notices replace annual bias audits

**California AI Laws (effective Jan 1, 2026):**
- AB 853 (AI Transparency Act): Labeling, provenance, disclosure requirements
- SB 53 (Transparency in Frontier AI Act): Risk governance requirements
- **Impact on Starting 5:** Applies regardless of company location if CA users are served

**Federal Preemption Push:**
- White House March 2026: Called on Congress to preempt state AI laws
- Would override CO, CA, UT, TX state AI laws if enacted
- Unlikely to pass in current form; state laws remain operative
