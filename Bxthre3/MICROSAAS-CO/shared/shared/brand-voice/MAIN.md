# Brand Voice — MicroSaaS Ventures

## Core Voice Attributes
- **Direct** — Say what you mean. No fluff.
- **Honest** — Don't oversell. Show working product.
- **Practical** — Talk in terms of time saved, money made, problems solved.
- **Human** — Not robotic, not corporate. Real people, real valley.

## Tone Rules
| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Marketing copy | Confident, benefit-led, action-oriented |
| Support responses | Calm, solution-first, warm but not chatty |
| Sales outreach | Helpful, consultative, never pushy |
| Social media | Conversational, local flavor, occasional humor |
| Legal/compliance | Precise, zero ambiguity |

## Must-Never-Do List
- Never use "leverage", "synergy", "ecosystem", "best-in-class"
- Never use ALL CAPS except in actual alerts
- Never write more than 3 sentences without a paragraph break
- Never lie about features, timelines, or pricing

**Voice samples:**
- "Estimate in 5 minutes. Close the job same day."
- "Stop texting yourself reminders. Let the app track everything."
- "Your truck's messy, your phone's cracked, your quotes are still prettier than your competitor's."

### TradeDesk
**Tone:** No-nonsense operator. Work smarter.
**Voice samples:**
- "Run your crew without running yourself ragged."
- "Your office called. AI answered."
- "Jobs scheduled, invoices sent, customers happy. While you were sleeping."

### AgentDeploy
**Tone:** Friendly futurist. Simple power.
**Voice samples:**
- "One agent. One role. Deployed today."
- "Like having an employee who never sleeps, never calls in sick, and never forgets."
- "Tell it what to know. Point it at your tools. Go."
