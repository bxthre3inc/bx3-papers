# PaintPro — Product Brief

## What It Is
White-label mobile app enabling local painting contractors to generate instant, professional estimates using their phone camera. AR overlay measures rooms automatically. Customer receives quote via SMS/email in under 5 minutes.

## Target Customer
- Local painting contractors (1-10 employees)
- DIY homeowners doing room repaints
- Property managers managing multiple units

## Key Features
1. Camera capture room photos → AR tape measure overlay
2. Auto square footage calculation (walls + ceiling)
3. Paint type selection (eggshell, satin, semi-gloss, etc.)
4. Labor estimate based on sq ft + complexity
5. Materials list + cost estimate
6. Quote PDF generation
7. SMS/email quote delivery
8. Customer e-signature acceptance
9. Review request post-estimate

## Pricing
| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | $29/mo | 20 estimates/mo, white-label |
| Pro | $99/mo | Unlimited estimates, priority support, analytics |

## Primary Objections + Responses
- "I already give written estimates" → Save 20 min/estimate. Client gets quote in 5 min, not 2 days.
- "It's too expensive for just one guy" → At $99/mo, one new job/month covers it. Your time is worth $75/hr.
- "How accurate is it?" → Within 5% of manual tape measure. Built on ARKit/ARCore.
- "Will my customers need an app?" → No. Customer receives a shareable link + PDF.

## Competitors
- Jobber (too complex, too expensive)
- Housecall Pro (dental-office pricing)
- HomeAdvisor (lead gen fees, not software)

## Unique Angle
"Estimate in 5 minutes. Close the job same day."
