# Objection Handling Library — Sales

## Universal Objections

### "I need to think about it"
**Response:** "Totally. What specifically are you weighing? I want to make sure I address the real concern rather than rush you."

### "Send me more info"
**Response:** "Happy to. What's the best email to send it to, and when should I follow up — later this week or next? [Note in CRM]"

### "We're not ready / no budget right now"
**Response:** "Completely understand. What would need to change for this to become a priority — better timing, approval, or budget? [Set follow-up reminder in CRM]"

### "Your competitor is cheaper/better"
**Response:** "Makes sense. Can you help me understand what you're currently using and what's missing? I want to compare honestly, not just pitch."

### "I don't trust AI"
**Response:** "Fair concern. The way to think about it: this isn't AI replacing you, it's AI handling the admin work so you can focus on the actual job. Would a free test-run help you see it in action?"

## Product-Specific Objections

### PaintPro
- "I already use a notepad" → "What does your current estimate-to-close rate look like? PaintPro cuts estimate time from 45 min to 5 min — our users close 30% more jobs by being faster."
- "Will it work on old phones?" → Android 10+ / iOS 14+ covered. Most contractors upgrade every 3 years anyway."
- "Can I customize the quote format?" → Yes — logo, colors, terms. Pro tier includes full PDF customization."

### TradeDesk
- "My guys won't learn new tech" → "Set-and-forget. The AI answers the phone, texts customers, and texts YOU only when human judgment is needed. Your crew doesn't touch software."
- "What if it gives wrong info?" → "Guardrails built in. It reads from your knowledge base only. If it doesn't know, it escalates to you."
- "How does dispatch work?" → "AI learns your routes, job types, and crew availability. You approve or adjust each morning."

### AgentDeploy
- "We tried chatbots and it was a disaster" → "This isn't a chatbot. It's a dedicated employee with a name, your knowledge, and your tools. Test it with your actual customer FAQ for 14 days free."
- "What if it says something wrong?" → "Content guardrails + approval workflow. You set what it can/cannot say. Human review on sensitive topics."
- "How is this different from ChatGPT?" → "This has memory, your tools, your knowledge base, and works autonomously. Not a one-shot chat."

### Special Delivery
- "Instacart/DoorDash already here" → "We're local-first for SLV. We know the roads, the stores, the drivers. National platforms ignore us. We're built for the Valley."
- "How do I know drivers are safe?" → "Background-checked, ID-verified, real-time photo on delivery. Every order insured up to $500."
- "What if my order is wrong?" → "$20 instant refund, no questions, credited to your account. Driver gets corrected in real time."

### SLV Ride
- "Uber/Lyft are everywhere" → "Not in SLV. Try getting a ride after 9pm in Center or Del Norte. We're the backbone."
- "Is it safe?" → "SafeRide verification: driver background check, vehicle inspection, ride tracking, panic button, and verified driver photos."
