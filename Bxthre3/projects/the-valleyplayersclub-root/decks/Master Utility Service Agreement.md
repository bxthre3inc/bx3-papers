## 1. Scope of Service: Fidelity-on-Demand (FoD)

The Company agrees to provide **On-Demand Fidelity Provisioning (OFP)**
through the **Foundry Protocol**. This service is a utility-based access
model for systemic certainty, enabling the Subscriber to transition
between probabilistic estimates and deterministic resource management.

### 1.1 Provisioning Tiers

The Subscriber is granted access to the following Utility Tiers as
governed by the **Agentic Operating Layer (AOL)**:

-   **Observational Utility:** Access to spatial grids (50m/20m
    > baseline; 1m via \"Resolution Pop\").

-   **Cognitive Utility:** High-frequency Bayesian modeling and
    > Kriging-weighted predictive trends.

-   **Actuation Utility:** Deployment of the **Deterministic Execution
    > Manifest (DEM)** for autonomous edge control.

## 2. The \"Resolution Pop\" Protocol

The \"Resolution Pop\" is defined as a **Systemic Resource Request**
initiated either by the Subscriber (via UI) or by **Agentic** (via
autonomous logic).

-   **Trigger:** A \"Pop\" occurs when the delta between *available*
    > fidelity and *required* fidelity for a mission-critical operation
    > exceeds the current Tier.

-   **Billing:** Each \"Resolution Pop\" event consumes **Fidelity
    > Credits** as defined in the Subscriber's specific Tiered Pricing
    > Addendum.

## 3. Regulatory Compliance & IRP Ledger

For the purpose of the **June 29th Water Trial** and subsequent
regulatory filings:

-   **Immutable Resource Provenance (IRP):** The Company shall maintain
    > a hashed, tamper-evident ledger of all **Manifest Intents** and
    > **Local Executions**.

-   **Quantifiable Conservation Validation (QCV):** Upon completion of a
    > logic cycle, the Company will provision a QCV Report, certifying
    > the \"Proof of Savings\" based on the deterministic record of the
    > IRP Ledger.

-   **Asynchronous Autonomy:** The Subscriber acknowledges that the
