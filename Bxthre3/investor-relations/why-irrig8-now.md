## The Problem Nobody Is Solving

Only **22% of global cropland is irrigated**, yet it produces **40% of the world's food**. In water-constrained regions—places like Colorado's San Luis Valley—every drop saved equals more crop per unit of land, more yield per acre-foot of water, and more survival margin for family farms navigating a drying West.

The precision irrigation market is fragmented, sensor-first, and disconnected from the regulatory reality farmers face. Most "smart irrigation" products are WiFi-connected apps with optimistic UX mockups. They don't have forensic audit trails. They can't prove water savings in court. They don't integrate with water rights adjudication. They aren't built for the lawyer-heavy, rights-adjacent reality of Western agriculture.

**That's the gap Irrig8 was built to close.**

## What Irrig8 Actually Is

Irrig8 is a **deterministic farming operating system** for center-pivot irrigation in water-constrained regions. It fuses:

- **Satellite data** (NDVI, moisture mapping)
- **On/inset-ground sensor suites** (soil moisture, ET, temperature)
- **Soil variability maps** (1-meter resolution from SLV research)
- **Neighboring system data** (shared telemetry, regional baselines)
- **External APIs** (weather, water rights registries, market pricing)

Into a single **10-Point Reality Vector** per zone—spatial (X, Y), temporal (T), vertical (Z: -100cm to +10m), certainty (C), logic state (L), fidelity (F), economic value (E), governance (G)—that drives **deterministic watering decisions**.

Not probabilistic. Not AI-generative. **Rule-based, auditable, court-admissible.**
