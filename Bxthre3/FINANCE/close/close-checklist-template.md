# Monthly Close Checklist — Bxthre3 Inc
**Target: Day 5 of Each Month**

---

## Close Month: ___________ | Close Date: ___________ | Prepared By: Recon

---

## Pre-Close (Day 1)

- [ ] Verify bank statement downloads complete for all accounts
- [ ] Download credit card statements (all corporate cards)
- [ ] Confirm payroll entries posted (Gusto)
- [ ] Verify AP batch posted in Bill.com
- [ ] Confirm all invoices posted in Stripe
- [ ] Review and post any accrued expenses
- [ ] Verify intercompany transactions are balanced

---

## Day 2 — Reconciliation

### Bank Reconciliations
- [ ] Chase operating account reconciled
- [ ] Chase savings/eagle account reconciled
- [ ] All corporate card accounts reconciled
- [ ] PayPal / merchant accounts reconciled
- [ ] Stripe balance account reconciled

### AR Reconciliation
- [ ] AR subledger ties to GL
- [ ] Unpaid invoices aging reviewed
- [ ] Collections notes updated
- [ ] Bad debt provision calculated

### AP Reconciliation
- [ ] AP subledger ties to GL
- [ ] Bill.com batch reviewed and posted
- [ ] Outstanding checks reviewed
- [ ] Prepaid expense schedules updated

---

## Day 3 — Account Review

### Balance Sheet Accounts
- [ ] Cash accounts reconciled and verified
- [ ] Accounts Receivable reconciled
- [ ] Accounts Payable reconciled
- [ ] Accrued liabilities reviewed
- [ ] Prepaid expenses amortized
- [ ] Fixed assets schedule updated
- [ ] Accumulated depreciation reviewed
- [ ] Equity accounts reviewed (cap table posting)

### Income Statement Review
- [ ] Revenue recognized per policy
- [ ] COGS entries complete
- [ ] Payroll entries complete and reconciled
- [ ] Operating expense accounts reviewed
- [ ] Non-recurring items documented

---

## Day 4 — Financials & Review

- [ ] Draft P&L reviewed
- [ ] Draft Balance Sheet reviewed
- [ ] Cash Flow statement prepared
- [ ] Variance to budget analyzed
- [ ] Any unusual items documented
- [ ] Journal entries posted for adjustments
- [ ] Trial balance finalized

---

## Day 5 — Close & Deliver

- [ ] Final review with Ledger (Controller)
- [ ] Books closed in QuickBooks
- [ ] Close package delivered to Ledger
  - [ ] Finalized P&L
  - [ ] Finalized Balance Sheet
  - [ ] Cash Flow Statement
  - [ ] AP Aging Summary
  - [ ] AR Aging Summary
  - [ ] Reconciliation schedules
  - [ ] Journal entry log
  - [ ] Accrual schedules
- [ ] Close notes documented
- [ ] Next month prep initiated
