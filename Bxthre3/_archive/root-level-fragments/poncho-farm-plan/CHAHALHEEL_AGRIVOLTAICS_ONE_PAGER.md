# Chahalheel Enterprise — Agrivoltaics One-Pager

**Bison Ranch + Solar Dual-Use | San Luis Valley, Colorado**

---

## COMPANY

- **Entity:** Chahalheel Enterprise LLC
- **Co-Founders:** Poncho (Navajo Nation operator) + Shasta
- **Location:** Monte Vista / Saguache County, San Luis Valley, Colorado
- **Stage:** Phase 1 — Land, LLC & Funding

---

## THE OPPORTUNITY

- 2,000–2,500 acres of high-altitude agricultural land in Colorado's San Luis Valley
- Dual-use model: solar energy generation + bison ranching (solar grazing)
- USDA REAP grant pathway covers 50% of ~1MW solar array cost
- Bison underlay generates $50K–$150K/yr in solar grazing revenue

---

## THE MODEL

**Phase 1 (Months 0–18)**
Execute operator agreement, form Chahalheel LLC, secure land lease, verify water rights, file Section 17 petition, acquire ~500 bison starter herd, deploy REAP-funded solar array, mobile slaughter unit

**Phase 2 (Months 18–36)**
Section 17 conversion → Navajo Nation federal corporation charter, expand herd to 1,000 head, establish direct-to-consumer bison sales

**Phase 3 (Years 3–5)**
Scale to 2,000-head herd, full solar grazing operation at scale, $500K+ annual revenue target
