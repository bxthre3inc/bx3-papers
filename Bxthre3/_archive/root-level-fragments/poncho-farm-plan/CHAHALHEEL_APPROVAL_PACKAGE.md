## Upon Approval — Automatic Actions

1. **Operator Agreement Signed** — DocuSign within 24 hours (Poncho employment + conversion protection clause)
2. **Chahalheel LLC Formed** — Colorado Articles of Organization, EIN, operating agreement (48–72 hrs)
3. **Bxthre3 Services Agreement Activated** — Deferred invoicing at $5,000/mo, zero payment until first grant funds hit LLC account
4. **Section 17 Petition Filed** — Navajo Nation Council submission prepared and routed for digital signature
5. **Grant Applications Queued** — NAAF ($500K), REAP ($500K), First Nations Oweesta ($250K) — pre-populated and ready to file the moment LLC is active
6. **Land Lease Process Initiated** — Target properties contacted, lease with purchase option presented via DocuSign
